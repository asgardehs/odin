# Product Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP

- Facility / Establishment management
- Employee management
- Incident logging with root cause tracking and the three routing
  axes (HazardType, ActionContext, ContextualConditions)
- OSHA 300 / 300A / 301 export via the ITA CSV format
- Chemical inventory
- Training tracking with alerts when training is coming due
- Permits and fees
- Schema builder for custom tables
